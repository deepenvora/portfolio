About me

I'm Deepen, Product Design Lead with 16+ years of experience working across Healthcare, Fintech, Ecommerce, and Travel in B2B and B2C companies. My role has encompassed a wide range of responsibilities — from conceptualisation and design strategy to project execution and delivery.

I've evolved from crafting screens to leading design modules, mentoring teams, and building design systems that scale.

Today, I balance leadership and hands-on design, while leveraging AI-enhanced workflows to speed iteration and focus on creativity.

Most recently at Famli, a FinTech wealth management platform digitising alternative investment transactions, I was leading design — shaping complex financial workflows into intuitive, scalable interfaces.

At my core, I believe in turning ambiguity into clarity — creating experiences that are intuitive for users and valuable for businesses.
